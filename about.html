Hi, I'm Shaurya

I'm 14 and I make stuff. A lot of stuff.

Games, apps, gadgets, random experiments — on a screen or in real life, if it's buildable I want to build it. Here's a little more about me, the stuff I love, and my (very correct) opinions.

My story

It started with taking things apart.

I've always wanted to know how stuff works — so I'd take it apart, poke at it, and (usually) put it back together. That turned into building my own things — little games and apps, sure, but also stuff I can actually hold: gadgets, contraptions, and projects that started as "wouldn't it be cool if…"

Now I basically can't stop. I love the moment an idea in my head becomes something real — whether that's on a screen or sitting right in front of me on my desk. I'm still learning tons, and that's honestly my favorite part.

When I'm not building, I'm probably gaming, messing with tech, or arguing about my hot takes.
